NEW DUBLIN DRAGONS

This is a project that I did for personal fun. I have always wanted to design a logo or jersey for a team. I started this project using my first elementary school's team name - The New Dublin Dragons. I began sketching the dragon and brought a more aggressive and modern version than New Dublin's initial logo. I changed the colour logo's colour from the green to to red and found that it helped make it more menacing. I finalized a sketch, and produced my first draft of the digital version. I made some small changes from the sketch, and I was very happy with the first draft. I then looked for ways to improve it by asking some friends and family. I used geometry and perfect circles to form the foundation of the revised logo and produced the second version. From there, I created a mockup of what it would look like on a jersey, and as a final hockey uniform.
