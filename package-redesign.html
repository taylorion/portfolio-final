Package Redesign

For this assignment, I had to find a wasteful package design and find an innovative way to reduce the amount of waste produced from the product, while keeping an interesting design. I chose a USB multi-card reader as some come in boxes the size of 2 or 3 stacked DVDs! I immediately had the idea to use a cylindrical design, as it would be similar to the size of a toilet paper roll. I designed a template for the package design and tested the prototype with success. I had some slight revisions before I produced the final design, but it remained largely unchanged. With this package redesign, I succeeded in reducing waste and creating an innovative new package.
